AprilTag range calibration

The Pi's range comes from solving the tag's geometry against the camera intrinsics. If those are slightly off (the default FOV-synthesized intrinsics often are), every reading is off by the same factor. Stand at a known distance, aim at the tag, type the actual distance below, and click Calibrate — the server solves the correction in one shot. Persists across restarts. Reset to 1.0 to remove all correction.
